Bill McDannold is the broker and owner of BnC Realty Group, an independent boutique brokerage based in Wellington, Florida. He represents buyers and sellers across Royal Palm Beach, Boynton Beach, Delray Beach, and the GL Homes Valencia communities that anchor Western Palm Beach County's 55+ market.

Bill works one-on-one. No team handoffs, no junior agent shuffle. Ninety closings on record, a 5.0 Zillow rating, a single closed $2.2M transaction this March, and a phone he picks up himself. The Valencia Cove buyer who wants a Charleston Grand on a lakefront pad in the Capri section gets Bill on tour day, not an intake form.
